Frequently Asked Questions

Find all the essential information you need in our FAQ section, designed to address the most frequently asked
questions and help you make informed decisions for your child's education.
What are the school hours at Little Learners Academy?

Our school hours are from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday to Friday. We also offer extended care options for parents who need early drop-off or late pick-up.

Is there a uniform policy for students?

Yes, we have a comfortable and simple uniform policy designed for ease and safety during school activities.

What extracurricular activities are available for students?

We offer arts, music, sports, language classes, and more to support holistic development.

What extracurricular activities are available for students?

We offer arts, music, sports, language classes, and more to support holistic development.

How do you handle food allergies and dietary restrictions?

We carefully follow all dietary guidelines and work closely with parents to address allergies or special dietary needs.

What is the teacher-to-student ratio at Little Learners Academy?

Our average teacher-to-student ratio is 1:8 for preschool and 1:12 for older children, ensuring personalized attention.

How do you handle discipline and behavior management?

We use positive reinforcement and age-appropriate guidance to promote respectful and responsible behavior.

How do I apply for admission to Little Learners Academy?

You can apply online via our website or visit our admissions office for more information and forms.
